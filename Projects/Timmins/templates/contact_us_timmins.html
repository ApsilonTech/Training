Frequently Asked Questions

GET TO KNOW OUR SERVICES

1. Can Timmins solutions be customized for my organization?
Yes, Timmins understands that every business is unique — with different people, work cultures, and goals. Hence, we are careful to offer only highly relevant and not ‘off-the-shelf’ or ‘general-purpose’ training, consulting, and staffing solutions. We work with your team every step of the way to ensure we meet all your upskilling, reskilling, hiring, onboarding, and upgrading needs in niche technology. Our customization process includes a needs assessment for your business.
2. Where does Timmins operate?
Timmins operates around the globe. Since our launch in 2015, we have catered to top global brands in the US, UK, Canada, Singapore, France, India, Israel, Malaysia, Indonesia, and China. Connect with us on +1 (888) 456 7890 for more details.
3. Does Timmins offer on-site training, consulting, and staffing services?
Timmins prioritizes safety. In view of the Covid-19 pandemic, we are currently offering only LIVE online training and consulting services to remote locations. We do, however, continue to offer on-site staffing services across the world, subject to the current national and international guidelines on travel, work, and other related Covid-19 rules and regulations.
4.What is Timmins refund policy?
Timmins values customer satisfaction. If you are not satisfied with any of our services, you can contact us at helpdesk@timmins-consulting.com to request a refund. After approval of your request, Timmins will ensure you receive a full refund within 30 days from the date of request. Please note: All requests for refunds are subject to the sole discretion of Timmins Training Consulting.
5.Is Timmins only a training provider?
Timmins is not just a training provider, but a niche technology partner that supports businesses across the globe to hire, reskill, upskill, hire, onboard, and upgrade in the latest technologies. We strive to always offer our clients world-class training solutions as well as premium staffing and consulting services in niche technology.
6. Has Timmins worked with diverse people groups?
At Timmins, we ensure that we develop all our training, consulting, and staffing solutions keeping your people, business, and workplace culture in mind. We strongly believe that people come before process and that diversity triggers innovation. This philosophy is reflected in our own team, which comprises subject matter experts from diverse backgrounds across borders.
